Fun Disguises

Point the camera at a face and dress it up. Glasses, hats, noses and more stick to faces by themselves and follow them as they move. An idea by Jane Yates. Nothing is recorded and nothing leaves your device.

The page starts with the selfie camera so you can disguise yourself. Tap the pieces at the bottom to put them on or take them off. On a video call, share this window with everyone and you will appear in disguise.
