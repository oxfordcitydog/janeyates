TV ONE

Fun Disguises

You appear on a vintage telly, and you dress up. Glasses, hats, beards, moustaches and noses stick to faces by themselves and follow them as they move. An idea by Jane Yates. Works on phones, tablets and computers. Nothing is recorded and nothing leaves your device.

The telly starts with its test card showing, then your camera appears on the screen. Tap the pieces at the bottom to put them on or take them off. If a piece sits a little off, press it and slide it into place. On a video call, turn your call camera off, share this window with everyone, and you will appear in disguise on an old television.
