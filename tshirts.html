T-Shirts

Wearable art editions — T-shirts printed with artworks from Blueprints of Technology, made to order in the UK and posted directly to you. Each is £200 including UK delivery (UK addresses only). Images below are low-resolution previews marked “SAMPLE”; the printed shirt carries the full artwork, no watermark.

How to order: press Buy, and when PayPal asks for a note, please include your size (S, M, L, XL or 2XL) and your UK delivery address. Please choose “goods and services” when paying. Each shirt is printed for you on demand, so please allow up to two weeks for delivery. Made-to-order items can be returned within 14 days of delivery if faulty or not as described.
